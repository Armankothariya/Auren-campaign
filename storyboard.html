Production Notes

Camera & Lens
  • Sony Venice 2 · 6K Full Frame
  • 35mm f/1.4 — hero and lifestyle
  • 120mm macro — detail shots
  • 50mm f/2 — rotation shot
  • Motorised slider for push-in
  • Remote head crane for eye-level
Lighting
  • Primary: Arri SkyPanel S60 soft key
  • Blue rim: Astera Titan tubes × 2
  • Product LED: practical built-in
  • Warm fill: Mole-Richardson 1K egg-crate
  • No hard sources — all diffused
  • LUX level: 2200 at product surface
Sound Design
  • Intro: Sub-bass 40Hz pulse × 3
  • Rotation: Whisper-wind ambient texture
  • Macro: Near-silence + breath sound
  • Lifestyle: Warm room tone, soft piano
  • Logo: Complete silence — intentional
  • Mix: Spatial stereo, subtle reverb tail
Grading & Post
  • DaVinci Resolve · ACES workflow
  • Teal shadow / blue midtone grade
  • S-curve contrast — no crushed blacks
  • LED bloom: Optical glow effect
  • Title card: Optically-printed grain
  • Deliverable: H.265 Master · ProRes 4444
