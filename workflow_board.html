Creative Workflow

The Process

From Concept to Campaign Delivery — Seven Stage Production

01
Phase One

Concept & Brand Identity

Established the complete brand architecture for AUREN: visual language, material hierarchy, lighting philosophy, and commercial positioning. Defined AUREN as a premium luxury audio brand — borrowing B&O's restraint, Apple's product clarity, and Dyson's engineering aesthetic.

Brand Architecture Visual Identity Colour System Typography Direction
Output: Complete AUREN brand style guide — palette, typographic scale, material references, and cinematic direction framework.
02
Phase Two

AI Generation & Iteration

Generated multiple headphone design concepts using structured prompt engineering — iterating on product geometry, material expression, and compositional arrangement. Each iteration evaluated against brand criteria: realism, commercial believability, and material consistency.

Prompt Engineering Multiple Iterations Lighting Tests Composition Studies
Output: 3 selected hero compositions from 40+ generated candidates. Selection based on material accuracy, lighting drama, and compositional strength.
03
Phase Three

Cleanup & Assembly

Systematic post-AI cleanup addressing all common generation artefacts: geometry inconsistencies at headband-cup junctions, uneven leather texture tiling, over-blurred LED ring edges, and background haze contamination. Each image assessed against a 12-point quality checklist.

Artefact Removal Geometry Correction Edge Refinement Inpainting
Output: Cleaned base images free of AI artefacts, with accurate product geometry, consistent material surfaces, and sharp, believable reflections.
04
Phase Four

Colour Grading & Cinematic Polish

Applied unified cinematic grading strategy across all campaign shots. Shadows pushed toward teal-blue to complement the AUREN LED signature. Midtones kept neutral-dark for material accuracy. Highlights permitted warm drift to contrast the cool chrome.

Teal-Orange Split Grade Selective Sharpening Contrast Architecture Adaptive Vignette
Output: Consistent cinematic look across all campaign assets with individual shot character preserved. LUT profile documented for production team reference.
05
Phase Five

Material Consistency Lock

Ensured identical headphone geometry, finish, and LED signature across all campaign assets. Absolute product integrity maintained across studio, lifestyle, and macro contexts. Cross-referenced all three shots against master product reference.

Geometry Lock Surface Match LED Calibration Cross-Reference
Output: Verified product consistency document — all shots aligned to master reference with zero deviation in material or form.
06
Phase Six

Reflection & Realism Correction

Corrected all reflective surfaces to achieve physical accuracy. Chrome bezel highlights aligned to actual light source positions. Obsidian surface reflections recalculated for correct angle of incidence. LED bloom controlled to prevent digital over-glow.

Reflection Physics Specular Accuracy Bloom Control Surface Realism
Output: Photorealistic reflection behaviour across all materials — believable under any lighting condition.
07
Phase Seven

Final Presentation & Delivery

Designed complete campaign presentation system using AUREN's typographic and spatial framework. Typography scaled to editorial luxury standards. Export prepared for digital, large-format print, and PDF review with colour-managed spaces and embedded ICC profiles.

Editorial Layout Typography System Export Preparation Colour Management
Output: Production-ready campaign boards, organised asset library, and complete deliverable package ready for client presentation.
