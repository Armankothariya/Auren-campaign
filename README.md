# AUREN

link: https://auren-campaign.vercel.app/

A cinematic luxury commercial microsite created for a Generative AI Creative Assignment.

Inspired by premium technology campaigns from brands like Apple, Bang & Olufsen, Sony, and Dyson, AUREN explores photorealistic AI-generated product imagery, cinematic storytelling, and modern creative workflows through a fictional high-end headphone brand.

---

## Live Experience

> Cinematic luxury campaign presentation showcasing:
- AI-generated commercial imagery
- Creative workflow direction
- Cinematic advertisement storyboard
- Post-production refinement process

# Workflow Overview

## 1. Concept Development

Defined a luxury technology brand identity with a cinematic visual language focused on minimalism, industrial precision, and premium acoustic design.

---

## 2. AI Image Generation

Generated campaign visuals using structured prompt engineering workflows emphasizing:

- Realistic lighting
- Premium materials
- Cinematic composition
- Consistent product geometry

---

## 3. Refinement & Cleanup

Performed post-production refinement including:

- Artifact cleanup
- Contrast balancing
- Cinematic grading
- Reflection enhancement
- Texture sharpening

---

## 4. Storyboarding

Designed a cinematic 15-second commercial direction including:

- Hero reveal
- Orbital movement
- Macro detailing
- Reflective product cinematography
- Luxury title reveal
